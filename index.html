Training

Prior to being an Orthopaedic surgeon he trained and practiced as a physiotherapist and has a broad knowledge of rehabilitation and exercise therapy. He then studied Medicine at The University of Sydney and pursued specialist training in Orthopaedic surgery after graduating. He trained in multiple major Sydney hospitals including Liverpool, St George, Prince of Wales and Sydney Hand Hospitals. After being accepted as a Fellow of the Royal Australasian College of Surgeons and a Fellow of the Australian Orthopaedic Association he did further subspecialty training in shoulder and elbow surgery as well as local and international training in hand and wrist surgery.


Profile

Dr Alan Dao is a very personable surgeon who takes pride in quality of care for all his patients. He adopts an evidence based approach to the investigation and management of his patient’s conditions and uses modern and well proven surgical techniques and implants. He specialises in minimally invasive as well as open surgery to shoulder, elbow, wrist and hand conditions. He treats conditions ranging from sports injuries and trauma to arthritis and degenerative conditions. He also has a strong interest in research and teaching. He has published in multiple local and international peer-reviewed journals as well as presenting a multiple local and international conferences. He is involved in teaching and education of allied health, nursing, medical students, general practitioners and orthopaedic specialist trainees. Dr Alan Dao is fluent in English and Cantonese.


Places of Practice

Dr Alan Dao has practices in Bankstown and Randwick and perform surgeries at Prince of Wales (Public & Private), Bankstown (Public) and Strathfield Private Hospitals.

 

Patient Information

For the first appointment please arrive 15 minutes early to allow time to complete the patient information health questionnaire.

What to bring:

  • Referral letter
  • Any prior operation reports or discharge summaries
  • Any x-rays, CT scans, MRI scans, Ultrasounds, bone scans and nerve conduction studies
  • Your Medicare and private health insurance details
  • Workers compensation or 3rd party details
  • Bring a trusted person with you to the consultation. This can often help with any questions you may have
  • Please wear appropriate clothing that will enable easy examination of your affected limb

What to expect:

  • On your first visit, Dr. Dao will spend 30min with you, obtaining a detailed history of your problem and performing a focused examination. He will then review any imaging you may have brought with you.
  • If appropriate, he may send you for further imaging or physiotherapy.
  • If he thinks an operation will help you, he will discuss this with you at length.
